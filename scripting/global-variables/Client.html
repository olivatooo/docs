Examples¶

Lua

on("MouseUp", function(KeyName, MouseX, MouseY)
    if (KeyName == "LeftMouseButton")
        Package:Log("Left Click pressed at X: " .. MouseX .. ", Y: " .. MouseY)
    end
end)
